Bharathi Education Trust (BET)

The Bharathi Education Society which subsequently became the Bharathi Education Trust has its headquarters at BET campus, Bharathinagara, MaddurTaluk, Karnataka. It was founded in 1962 by Sri G. Madegowda, the honourable ex-member of parliament and former minister, Govt. of Karnataka. It is aimed at fulfilling all the educational, cultural, social and other aspirations of the people in and around Bharathinagara, which was then a small hamlet of no significance.

Sri G. Madegowda is the Founder Chairman of the Trust and his sincere, unrelenting efforts have led to the establishment of several institutions at Bharathinagara, Hanumanthanagara and Kirugavalu under the auspices of B.E.T. The founder secretary of the Trust late Sri AnnurS.Siddegowda was also instrumental in setting up several institutions run by the Trust. The B.E.T. is now managed by trustees. The office-bearers of the Trust are:

  • Dr. G. Madegowda - Chairman
  • Sri Madhu G. Madegowda - Executive trustee
  • Sri B.M Nanjegowda - Secretary (Bharathinagara Campus)
  • Sri Siddegowda - Secretary (Hanumanathanagara Campus)
  • Sri B. Basavaraju - Working President
  • Sri K.B. Mahadevappa - secretary (Kirugavalu Campus

Bharathinagara Campus:

This is a sprawling campus of twenty six acres of lush green, located in the rural heartland of Mandya District. It is the nerve Centre of all the activities of B.E.T. It has a host of several institutions each complementing the other.

How to Reach: Bharathi Education Trust is situated in Bharathinagara (KM Doddi), MaddurTaluk, Mandya District, Karnataka. It is just 90 kms far from Bengaluru. First you have to reach Maddur on Bengaluru-Mysore (SH-7) highway and then the Institute is just 10 kms far from Maddur on Maddur-Malavalli road. It is just 60kms from Mysore. First you have to reach Mandya on Bengaluru-Mysore (SH-7) highway and then the Institute is just 20kms far from Mandya on Mandya-Malavalli road. Bharathi Education Trust has kept pace with the changing times and is now geared-up to provide skill based educational courses enabling the students to take up jobs in emerging fields like IT and BT. The campus has students coming from all over India for the various courses on offer. To meet the needs of the student’s two separate well equipped hostels for men and women are maintained by the Trust. The hostels have separate facilities for North- Indian and South-Indian students.

The campus at Bharathinagara also has a big playfield and an open-air theatre where students get ample opportunities to hone their skills. A unique feature of the campus is the “Gandhi Bhavana” which has a collection of rare photographs, depicting the life of the Mahathma. The building houses a library and a conference hall. It is in keeping with the pronounced goal of B.E.T. to provide value based education at an affordable cost and inculcating among students Gandhian values.

Hanumanthanagara Campus

Hanumanthanagara campus is situated four kilometres from Bharathinagara, spread over fifty acres of verdant landscape. The heart of the campus is the temple of Lord Athmalingeswara with its unique modern architecture and modernity. Nearby is the Bharathi Residential Composite Pre-University College. The place is ideally suited for meditation as well as learning. The serene atmosphere of the place contributes to the overall well-being of the students here. The campus also has a primary school where children from the surrounding villages pursueeducation. The Hanumanthanagara campus is the total expressionof the vision of Dr. G. Madegowda. The temple is theculmination of the spiritual aspirations of the people and the PrakruthiArogyaDhama which meets the healthneeds of people in the naturopathic way is perfectly in-keeping with the ideals of Bharathi Education Trust of providing good education for all at affordable rates, taking care of the health of people and also of providing foodwhere necessary.

Kirugavalu Campus

This campus is located in MalavalliTaluk of Mandya Dt. It is fifteen kilometres from Malavalli Town. B.E.T. has established a Pre-University College & a First Grade College here and a college of vocational Education, serving the educational needs of the people of the surrounding fifty villages B.E.T. has many plans on the anvil to improve the campus at Kirugavalu
