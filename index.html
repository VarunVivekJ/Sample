Welcome to GMIT, Mandya

G Madegowda Institute Of Technology was established in the year 2011 under the aegis of Bharati Educational Trust. Currently college provides 5 undergraduate and 2 post graduate courses in engineering. College has taken leading role in raising the entrepreneurial culture in the Mandya District. College has a campus sprawling over 20 acres of land. College is affiliated to VTU and is approved by AICTE, New Delhi. College is also recognized by Govt. Karnataka.
