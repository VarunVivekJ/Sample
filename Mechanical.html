About Department

G Madegowda Institute of Technology [GMIT] was established in the year 2011. The department of Mechanical Engineering was also established in the same year with an intake of 60. Department is composed of enriched faculty members qualified in various discipline with enormous knowledge, dedicated for the growth of students. Our department has well advanced equipments with skilled instructors and spacious laboratories.
The Department of Mechanical Engineering gives priority to update the Mechanical Engineering education, primarily driven by the dynamics of technological advances and sustainable development and the curriculum is revised on a continuous basis with active involvement of industry, alumni, research and organizations.
The department feels proud about producing responsible Mechanical Engineering graduates to serve the Nation. Over these glorious years of its journey, it has carved a niche for itself in the key areas of teaching, research, consultancy, administration and community services. In the year of 2014 -15 the department extended its intake to 120 and also offers M.Tech. in Product Design and Manufacturing with an intake of 18.

Vision

To be an Engineering Institute of highest repute and produce world- class engineers catering to the needs of mankind.
To develop the department as a centre of excellence in education, research & entrepreneurship by providing depth of knowledge in mechanical engineering & build professional ethics by dedicated faculty, facility & infrastructure.

Mission

  • 1. To equip students with good analytical ability, practical knowledge with strong theoretical knowledge and value system to become high calibre mechanical engineers and leaders.
  • 2. To inspire and develop innovative ideas, decision is making & practical problem solving skill.
  • 3. To encourage students to achieve superior proficiency & imbibe professional ethics.
  • 4. To develop students potential through high quality teaching-learning process and state of the art infrastructure.
  • 5. To promote research & development activities and entrepreneurial qualities.
