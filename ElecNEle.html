About Department

Electrical and Electronics Engineering department was started during the year 2011 with a sanctioned intake of 60 students. The department has a state of art of infrastructure which also caters VTU requirements. Department is fully equipped with all equipment in the various laboratories viz Measurements & Circuit simulation lab, Transformer &Induction machine lab, DC machine & Synchronous machine lab, Electronics lab, Op-Amps lab, CAED lab, Control System lab, and Power Simulation lab. The department has licensed version MI-POWER software, CADE software and Pspice software. The department has 11 faculties one Professor & H.O.D and ten Assistance professors. Faculties possess required qualification as per AICTE norms. Department conducts every year National conferences, Technical talk and also arrange for industrial visits to the students of 3rd and 4th year. Department conducts “Electrical wiring and Grounding” and “Build my ROBO” workshops for the benefits of EEE & other branch students also. Besides this department conducts Faculty Development Program to the newly joined staffs. Weekly academics, Extra curriculum activities conducted by the department for 2nd and 3rd year students.

Vision

To achieve academic excellence in Electrical & Electronics Engg. , thus enabling the students to have enhanced opportunity in the involving global industrial scenario. To develop Electrical & Electronics Engg. Department into a centre of academic excellence which will produce quality Engineers who can face the scientific & Technological challenges with creativity & Innovations.

Mission

To produce competent and disciplined Electrical & Electronics Engineers through delivery of quality education to meet the ongoing global challenges in alignment with technical Education system and society.
