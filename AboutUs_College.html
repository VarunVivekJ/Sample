G. Madegowda Institute of Tecnology (GMIT)

G Madegowda Institute of Technology (GMIT) one of the premier institution started in the year 2011 with CSE, EEE, ECE, Civil and Mechanical branches by Bharathi Education Trust (BET), Bharathinagar. Since then, it has grown with time. At present, the institution runs 5 under graduate courses in Bachelor of Engineering (BE) 2 Post graduation courses in master of technology ( M.Tech) with an annual intake of about 415 students. The institution began with the mission of preparing the young generation for “Integration & Prosperity”. Today more than thousand students are studying in this institute & they are guided by more than 60 well qualified & experienced faculty members who work to integrate the mission of the college by their teaching.

Bharathi Education Trust (BET)

Chairman's Message

Dear Students,
Bharathi Education Trust (BET) was established in 1962 with the purpose of providing quality education in rural areas at an affordable cost. The goals and objectives of Bharathi Education Trust are three folds-Education in rural areas, providing safe health care facilities and providing food for the needy and the deserving. The Trust has grown over the years into a host of institutions starting from the Pre-primary School to Post-graduate courses. B.E.T. has many more endeavors on hand. In 2011 we have started G Madegowda Institute of Technology (GMIT) with five courses to provide the technical education to all sorts of students. I wish our students would realize the importance of learning in a rural ambience and
I wish you all success
Dr. G Madegowda M.A., LLB., Chairman, BET
