G. Madegowda Institute of Tecnology (GMIT)

G Madegowda Institute of Technology (GMIT) one of the premier institution started in the year 2011 with CSE, EEE, ECE, Civil and Mechanical branches by Bharathi Education Trust (BET), Bharathinagar. Since then, it has grown with time. At present, the institution runs 5 under graduate courses in Bachelor of Engineering (BE) 2 Post graduation courses in master of technology ( M.Tech) with an annual intake of about 415 students. The institution began with the mission of preparing the young generation for “Integration & Prosperity”. Today more than thousand students are studying in this institute & they are guided by more than 60 well qualified & experienced faculty members who work to integrate the mission of the college by their teaching.

Bharathi Education Trust (BET)

The Bharathi Education Society which subsequently became the Bharathi Education Trust has its headquarters at BET campus, Bharathinagara, Maddur Taluk, Karnataka. It was founded in 1962 by Sri G. Madegowda, the honourable ex-member of parliament and former minister, Govt. Of Karnataka. It is aimed at fulfilling all the educational, cultural, social and other aspirations of the people in and around Bharathinagara, which was then a small hamlet of no significance. There are 14 Institutions under BET and more than 10,000 students are studying in this campus. Sri G. Madegowda is the Founder Chairman of the Trust and his sincere, unrelenting efforts have led to the establishment of several institutions at Bharathinagara, Hanumanthanagara and Kirugavalu under the auspices of B.E.T. The founder secretary of Trust late Sri Annur S.Siddegowda was also instrumental in setting up several institutions run by the Trust.

Chairman's Message

The goals and objectives of Bharathi Education Trust are three folds-Education in rural areas, providing safe health care facilities and providing food for the needy and the deserving.
