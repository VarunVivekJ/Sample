Placement Cell

Placement @ G Madegowda Institute of Technology
Training and Placement Department is established as an integral part of the College with an active role in career services for its Students. The Department is headed by Mr. Mohinder N Raj, Incharge Training & Placement Officer. He is supported by staff coordinators and student co-ordinators from each department. The vital role of T&P Cell is to groom & shape the students and make them industry ready so that they can face the future challenges in the real world. Every student pursuing a professional career in engineering has an ambition to obtain a challenging job in a company of repute (a brand name) and make a successful career. The Department conducts various training sessions for students by eminent trainers from the industry and conduct workshops and seminar to enhance their skills.
