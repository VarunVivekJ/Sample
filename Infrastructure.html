Hostel

GMIT and BET Group offers separate hostels for Boys and Girls with well furnished rooms. We run our hostels in a green and peaceful environment which welcomes students. These hostels will accommodate more than 750 students comfortably. The hostel cleanliness comforts to ace hygiene standards. Mess facilities are also available to provide the hostilities with nutrition’s and quality food. Each room in all the hostels has been furnished with cots, tables, chairs, book racks and a ceiling fan.

Transportation

A full-fledged Transport department functions in the college with buses to provide transport facility to students and staff from various places.This service is offered ensuring a hassle-free and safe transportation. The College runs buses for providing transport facility to students from various places in and around the said cities. In addition to college transport public transport, private transport facility is also available as they ply very frequently on highways from all the places like Malavalli, Maddur and Mandya, with scheduled stop in front of college.
  • Route 1: Mandya – Madarahalli -- Bharathinagar
  • Route 2: Mysore – Bannur – Malavalli -- Bharathinagar
  • Route 3: Malavalli -- Bharathinagar
  • Route 4: Maddur -- Bharathinagar
  • Route 5: Mandya – Maddur -- Bharathinagar
  • Route 6: Mysore – Mandya -- Bharathinagar
